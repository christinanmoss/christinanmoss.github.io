About Me

I am a graduate student at the University of Pennsylvania, where I focus on urban technology and the transformative role data-driven solutions can play in shaping more equitable, efficient, and responsive cities. With a passion for bridging urban planning and emerging technologies, I explore how tools like geospatial analysis, AI, and smart infrastructure can inform better decision-making and drive meaningful change in the built environment. My work sits at the intersection of design, data, and policy, where innovation meets impact.
